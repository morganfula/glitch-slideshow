Shibuya City

Shibuya is a special ward in Tokyo, Japan. A major commercial and finance center, it houses the two busiest railway stations in the world, Shinjuku Station and Shibuya Station. As of May 1, 2016, it has an estimated population of 221,801 and a population density of 14,679.09 people per km².

Kamiyamachō

Kamiyama-chō (神山町) is a residential district of southwestern Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan. As of 1 October 2020, it has a population of 2,872 people.[1] Its postal code is 150-0047. Its nearest stations are Yoyogi-Hachiman, Yoyogi Kōen, Shinsen, Shibuya and Harajuku. Famous residents include former Japanese prime ministers Shigeru Yoshida and Tarō Asō.
